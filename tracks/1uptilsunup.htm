Trivia:

  • Although there was not actually a physical or digital "single" release, this song is still considered the lead single from 12 Step Program. A music video was made for the song, and it was available on iTunes ahead of the full album release.
  • This track has frequently been a part of PUTS' live performances since the release of 12 Step Program, and the song is often preceeded by Thes One projecting a quick snippet of Legend of Zelda gameplay onto a stage wall.

This track appears on the following releases:

  • 12 Step Program (2014) (LP, Cassette & CD) - PUTS Records (PL7015)
