43 Labels I Like

Written by: Michael Turner & Christopher Portugal

[Thes One sample:] Digging in the crates, every day of the month… (scratched and repeated)
Step back when I pull out the wax, blow dust…

Trivia:

  • The slowed-down Thes One samples at the beginning of this song are from the second verse of Live At The Fishbucket (Pt. 1).
  • Even though I'm still having a little trouble hearing some of the pre-list-off lyrics, I have at least confirmed all 43 record labels mentioned in the track by using the booklet from the 10 Years Of Thes One release. Thanks, Thes!
  • MAJOR big-ups to "LagParty" for the Spanish transcription at the end of the track. According to the homie, it translates to: "Today you arrived with the wind / With the wind of silence / I know you're there / That you're looking at me." Thanks, Lag!
