Trivia:

  • I've heard this track on a recorded show circa 2000 during their world tour with JazMak. The tape wasn't the best quality, which made it difficult to understand (hence all the grey text.) After Double's intro, he rocks the tables while Thes and JazMak do most of the MCing.
  • This track also preceded Live At The Fishbucket (Pt. 1) at the show I had taped, and JazMak's last line is cut off by Thes jumping on into the intro for Fishbucket. This live introduction to Fishbucket can also be heard at the beginning of the last track on American Men, Vol. 1: Live At Glastonbury.
