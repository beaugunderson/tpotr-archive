Trivia:

  • The working title for this song was "Camel Brains".
  • "…like Mr. Cooper, yo, we hanging" – A reference to the TV show, "Hangin' with Mr. Cooper".
  • "By the Time I Get to Phoenix" is a pop song, written in 1965 by Jimmy Webb and performed by Johnny Rivers. It is one of the most covered tracks in history, (Glen Campbell, Reba McEntire, Dean Martin, Isaac Hayes, Burl Ives, and Nick Cave and the Bad Seeds, among others)
  • "Barnes with Noble hooks" – May be a reference to Barnes & Noble bookstores
  • "Cris" refers to Cristal, a brand of high-end champagne
