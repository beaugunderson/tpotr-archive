Notes:
*The set is dated as a 2010 release, but it didn't actually see the light of day until 2013.
**The labels for Disc 4 were accidentally flipped, so according to the booklet, "Disc 4, Side A" should be "Side B" and vice-versa. For the purposes of this website, I am keeping them as they are labeled on the record (out of order).

Release versions:
Limited release 5xLP set in wooden crate: PL70 Records (2010/2013) - PL7001 (Only 150 made). Limited release MP3 Player: Only 190 released (150 with the Vinyl set PL7001, and 40 with a separate, limited-edition glass mug set).
