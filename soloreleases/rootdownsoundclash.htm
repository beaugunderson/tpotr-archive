Thes One Presents: Live At The Rootdown Soundclash (2004) (Thes One)

CD Release:

  1. Introduction
  2. The Baron (Theme)
  3. Want You Back / "Will.I.Am Takes His Shirt Off"
  4. Too Close
  5. Will Speaks
  6. Joints And Gyms
  7. Tribute To Weldon Irvine
  8. Everytime
  9. Pretty Girls
  10. The 6th Cup
  11. Up, Down And Away

Album info:
All tracks produced by Thes One, with small portions of material on tracks 1-3 & 5 by Will.I.Am.

Tracks 1-6: Recorded live at The Gabah in Los Angeles, CA, on April 25, 2002. This event was a beat battle billed as "The Baron Wolfgang von Thes vs. Will.I.Am", and it was hosted by DJ Dusk and Cut Chemist.
Tracks 7-11: Previously unreleased material made for the battle that wasn't used.

Release versions:
CD: P-Vine Records (2004) - PCD-4199 (Released in 2004).
